    Bees are at risk of extinction. This was revealed by the data made available by Ipbes, according to which the reduction of these and other pollinating insects is definitely worrying and the effects also on humans risk being catastrophic.

    Who will the money go to?
    The money donated will go entirely to the partner beekeepers of the project

    What will you get?
    You will have a personalized page where you can see where your money has gone, the location, the beekeeper, how much honey he produces, etc. Furthermore, as the project progresses, you will get one of the honey at home produced by the same bees that you saved!

    ATTENTION!!! At this moment the project is in alpha and it is not yet possible to register, however we will provide you with the promised page through a unique id sent by email (paypal)
